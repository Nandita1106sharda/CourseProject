About Us

Bhangra Beats was founded in 2001 by a group of passionate bhangra and giddha devotees, all of whom hav countless years of experience and knowledge under their belt. The joint passion of culture, heritage and musical art was combined to create this flourishing academy.

It is one of the best bhangra and gidha academies in British Columia as we have performed at competitions in client such as Los Angeles, Boston, Detroit, Clagary, Edmonton, Las Vegas, etc. We help our students build confidence, important life skills and lifelong relationships.
